Master the Arena

Welcome to the hub for Roblox Rivals, the fast-paced arena fighter by Fun Makers. Build your loadout, hone your skills, and battle for supremacy!

Join The Fight

Key Features

Rivals is a unique fighting game experience on Roblox, focusing on skill-based combat and strategic loadout variety.

Dynamic Combat System

Craft your perfect playstyle by combining different weapons with powerful scrolls, each granting unique abilities for endless strategic depth.

Multiple Game Modes

Test your skills in competitive 1v1 and 2v2 Ranked matches, or cause chaos in casual Team Deathmatch and Free-For-All modes.

Cosmetic Customization

Personalize your avatar with unique weapon skins, kill effects, emotes, and titles. Show off your achievements and style in the arena!

Master Your Arsenal

Your victory depends on the loadout you bring to the fight. Mix and match to find what works for you.

Melee Weapons

Swords, Katanas & More

Engage in fast-paced, close-quarters combat. Outmaneuver your opponents with quick strikes and powerful combos.

Ranged Weapons

Guns & Projectiles

Keep your distance and pick off foes with precision. Control the flow of battle from afar with a variety of firearms.

Powerful Scrolls

Game-Changing Abilities

Equip scrolls to unleash devastating special moves, from powerful ground slams to teleportation and protective barriers.

Strategic Movement

Dodge, Dash & Jump

Mobility is key. Master the art of dashing, double-jumping, and quick dodges to outplay your rivals and avoid danger.

High Skill-Ceiling Combat

Success in Rivals is determined by skill. Master movement, learn ability cooldowns, and combine your weapon and scroll abilities into powerful combos to dominate the leaderboard.

  • No Pay-to-Win: All gameplay-affecting items are earned. Purchases are for cosmetics only, ensuring a level playing field.
  • Ranked System: Compete in the ranked playlist to earn ELO, climb through divisions from Bronze to Grandmaster, and prove your skill.
  • Constant Evolution: The game receives regular updates with balance changes, new weapons, scrolls, and features based on community feedback.
